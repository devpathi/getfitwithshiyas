I coach clients both in person here in Bengaluru and online across the country. People often ask me
which is "better." The honest answer: they're both excellent — it comes down to *you*.

## Choose in-person if…

- You're a beginner and want hands-on form correction
- You stay accountable best when someone's in the room
- You're based in or near Indiranagar, Bengaluru
- You enjoy the energy of training alongside your coach

## Choose online coaching if…

- Your schedule is unpredictable and you train when you can
- You're outside Bengaluru
- You're self-motivated and just need the right plan + accountability
- You want a lower-cost way to work with an experienced coach

## What stays the same either way

A fully personalised plan, real progression, nutrition guidance, and a coach who actually checks in
on you. The delivery changes — the standard of coaching doesn't.
